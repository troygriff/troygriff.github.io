T-Mobile

In Store Experience






Project Overview




Background


T-Mobile's in store applications were siloed and the user experience was fragmented. The project focus was to completely redesign their e-Commerce, Retail, and Customer Care systems and websites into a new integrated system.

Details


Over the course of the project I worked closely with multiple client teams to understand and balance the needs of application users. I created designs that adhered to internal design pattern guidelines and met stakeholder approval.







Challenge


The challenge for our team was designing an experience for in store representatives with multiple lines. The legacy system was restricted to work only with a single line, meaning in store reps were having to constantly repeat tasks. Our goal was to make comparing information between phone lines simple for these representatives by redesigning the Usage Summary feature which shows data usage and charges.
